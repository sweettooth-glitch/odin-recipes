Garlic Croutons

Description

Homemade garlic croutons are super easy to make.

Crunchy and delicious they make a wonderful addition to soups and salads. Make extra for later as these can easily be stored in an airtight container.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups bread - cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tsp oil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Set aside
  2. Toss together the bread cubes and spices in a bowl.
  3. Toss in the oil until well distributed - make sure the bread is evenly moistened.
  4. Spread the bread cubes out evenly onto the baking sheet. Bake for 12-18 minutes, tossing a time or two. Time will vary based on freshness of bread and type of bread used.
